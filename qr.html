QR Code Pairing

Scan the QR code with WhatsApp to connect instantly
Click "Generate QR Code" to start

Fast and secure QR code generation for instant sessions

How to Use

  1. Click the "Generate QR Code" button
  2. Open WhatsApp on your phone
  3. Go to Settings → Linked Devices
  4. Tap "Link a Device" and scan the QR code
  5. Wait for the connection to establish
  6. Your session ID will be automatically sent to your WhatsApp
  7. Copy the session ID and use it in your DARK-BOT configuration
